Apple

An apple is a round fruit that grows on the apple tree and is a member of the pome fruit family. Apples are high in fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants.

Banana

A banana is a fruit that grows on a tropical plant and is a berry botanically. It's a popular, widely grown fruit that's a good source of potassium.

Cherry

A cherry is a small, round fruit that grows on a tree or bush. Cherries can be sweet or sour, and are often used in cooking.
