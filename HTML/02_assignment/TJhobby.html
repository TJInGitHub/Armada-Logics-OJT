Emulating: Playing Console Games on My Mobile Phone

Why I love it?

I love to emulate console games on my mobile phone as it allow me to play it without buying their respective consoles.

Through this you'll have a variety of games on different consoles you can easily enjoy through your mobile phone, only worrying about if you can play every games available as there are a lot to choose from.

List of Emulators

Here's a list of emulators I used to play console games. Note that there are also other emulators available that I didn't include in the list as I haven't used those yet.

  • PPSSPP for PSP Games
  • ePsxe for PS1 Games
  • My Boy! for GBA Games
  • Snes9x EX+ for SNES Games
  • Drastic for Nintendo DS Games
